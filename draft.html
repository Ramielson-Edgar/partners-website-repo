CashBack Terms and Conditions

Any customer who deposits $100 or more and completes at least 10 Standard lots of trading in a calendar month can participate in the cashback program.

Cashback Structure:

  • Customers who trade between 10 and 1,000 Standard lots in one calendar month will receive 0.5% cashback on their Cent account and a 5% rebate from the spread they paid for trading that volume on all other account types.

  • Customers who trade between 1,000 and 3,000 Standard lots in one calendar month will receive 1% cashback on their Cent account and a 10% rebate from the spread they paid for trading that volume on all other account types.

  • Customers who trade 3,000 Standard lots or more in one calendar month will receive 1.5% cashback on their Cent account and a 15% rebate from the spread they paid for trading that volume on all other account types.

  • Customers can withdraw their cashback only once per month, between the 1st and the 5th of each month. To receive cashback, customers must complete a full account verification.

  • Please note that the current cashback cannot be combined with any other AdroFx bonuses.

  • Positions with a duration of less than 5 minutes or with profit/loss values less than two typical spreads of the traded instruments will not be considered when calculating the cashback amount.
